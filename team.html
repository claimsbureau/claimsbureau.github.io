Our Team

Claims Bureau USA is unique and we are structured like no other investigative company in our industry. We have made a commitment to our clients and utilize our own company employees to conduct every investigation. This commitment has enabled our clients to expect and to receive the highest quality product possible.

Our team is comprised of the brightest and most creative investigators, interviewers and surveillance agents who are carefully selected, continuously trained and supervised and who utilize our company’s proven investigative techniques and strategies.

We currently employ 50 plus internal investigators in our corporate headquarters that are responsible for conducting investigations that take place prior to, in conjunction with, or in place of field investigation or surveillance. Their findings often assist our field investigators and surveillance agents with expanding an investigation or targeting surveillance in order to optimize results.

In addition to our 50 plus internal investigators, we employ experienced field investigators, surveillance agents and interviewers who are strategically located throughout the country, which enables us the ability to provide coverage across the United States at local billing rates.

As a company, we are licensed to operate everywhere we work. All of our employees are licensed or registered as required by each state and are fully insured. Whether you are in need of one investigation or involved in a project requiring hundreds, our team is built to accommodate your needs.

Established in 1956. Over 50 years of proven industry experience.
