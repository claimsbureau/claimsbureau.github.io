About Us

Our Team

The Claims Bureau team is comprised of some of the brightest and most creative investigators. They are carefully selected, continuously trained and supervised and utilize proven investigative techniques and strategies.

We are built like no other company and have over 50 in-house investigators in our corporate headquarters to save our clients’ both time and money. In-house investigations take place prior to, in conjunction with, or in place of field investigation or surveillance. This often helps targets the surveillance and optimize the results. Follow-up investigation is also conducted, if warranted, after review of the surveillance findings.

Whether you are in need of one investigation or involved in a project in need of hundreds, our team is built to accommodate any size job. Both Claims Bureau USA’s in-office and field investigative staff produce in-depth, quality investigations that provide answers to your questions.

Our experienced field investigators, surveillance agents and interviewers are strategically located, which enables us an opportunity to provide coverage across the United States at local billing rates.

Claims Bureau USA has made a commitment to the industry to utilize our own company W-2 employees to conduct every investigation, which enables our clients to expect and receive the highest quality product possible.

We are licensed where we work across the United States. All of our employees are also licensed or registered as required by each state and our fully insured.
