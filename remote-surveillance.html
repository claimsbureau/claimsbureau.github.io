Remote Surveillance

Utilizing remote camera or unmanned surveillance platforms, Claims Bureau USA puts you in the best position to obtain video and monitor activity levels in areas or situations where obtaining video is difficult or near to impossible.

A remote camera platform is placed in a legal manner near a residence or business for 24 hour coverage and can last up to several days at a time. These non-intrusive cameras are operated and monitored by trained and licensed investigators who can also be nearby and work in conjunction (similar to a two-person team) to follow the subject if they depart the residence.

Some successful cases have involved, but not limited to:

  • Rural areas
  • Working from home
  • Yard work
  • Moving scenarios
