About Us

History

Claims Bureau’s origin dates back to 1956 when it was originally organized under the name Casualty, Life and Surety Companies’ Claims Bureau Inc. It was one of the first insurance fraud investigation agencies servicing the insurance industry and covered the six New England states.

In 1995, Casualty, Life and Surety Companies’ Claims Bureau Inc. was purchased by long-time employee investigators of the company. It was at this time that Casualty, Life and Surety Companies’ Claims Bureau Inc. became Claims Bureau Inc.

In 2001, Claims Bureau Inc. began obtaining necessary licenses and incorporated throughout the United States. This opportunity enabled our company to utilize our proven formula and strategies on a national level. It was at this time that our company began servicing national insurance carriers and Claims Bureau Inc. consequentially evolved into Claims Bureau USA.

While our reach and our company continue to grow, we remain partners with our original clients and we have sustained the original principles that our company was built upon: Quality, Service and Integrity.
