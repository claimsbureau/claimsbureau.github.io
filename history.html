About Us

History

Claims Bureaus' origin dates back to 1956. It was originally organized under the name Casualty, Life and Surety Companies' Claims Bureau Inc. It was the first insurance fraud investigation agency servicing the insurance industry and covered the six New England states.

In 1995, the company was purchased by long-time employee investigators of the company, and became Claims Bureau Inc.

In 2001, the company became licensed throughout the entire United States and began servicing national Insurance carriers thus bringing our formula for success to a national level and consequentially evolving into Claims Bureau USA.

Today we are now the major vendor in the Life and Health field and have made a major impact in this industry in saving money for our clients in both investigative costs and reserve savings. We operate differently than most vendors and use our imagination and years of experience to get the truth on claims in an efficient manner.

By partnering closely with our clients and working with them to form a customized strategy, we have been able to provide them with investigative solutions that save them money and get better results. Our customer base continues to grow because the smart claims people recognize targeted detailed investigation is the way to spend your investigative dollars.

Today while we are still partnered with a lot of our original clients we continue to expand but have not lost sight of the original principles and work ethics that have taken us so far: quality, service and integrity.
