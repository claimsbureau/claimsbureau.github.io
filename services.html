Training & Support

Roundtables

Claims Bureau USA communicates effectively with all of its clients to ensure that objectives are defined and that there is a clear understanding of the scope of the assignment.

As part of that service we routinely takes part in roundtable discussions with carriers to triage claims and develop an effective and targeted plan of action for an investigation. A carrier's investigative budget is utilized wisely as a result of partnering closely with Claims Bureau USA. 

Partnering with Claims Bureau will assist in creating an appropriate plan of action for investigation and/or surveillance or ruling the need of surveillance out completely.

Staff Training/Presentations

As part of our value added service and as part of our overall partnership we often provide Fraud awareness training to claims staff of our clients. This frequently produces and uptick in fraud referrals and ultimately saving our clients’ money.

We also provide investigative training that assist both claims and SIU staff on the latest in desktop, internet and social media investigative techniques they can conduct on their own from their desktop.
