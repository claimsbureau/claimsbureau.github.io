Careers

We are accepting resumes throughout the United States from qualified, enthusiastic individuals who wish to join the Claims Bureau USA team.

We provide both the training and the tools necessary for our investigators to succeed.

Candidates must be suitable for licensure or registration under our company license.

Because we expect the best, we provide a higher standard of compensation, which in turn leads to long-term career employees.

We are a growing company that recognizes achievement and promotes from within.


Our Investigators must posses:

  • A college degree or prior investigative experience
  • Good moral character
  • Integrity
  • A commitment to excellence
  • Orientation to detail
  • A reliable, low-profile vehicle
